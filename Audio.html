What did I hear when I visited?

During my visits, I recall the park being very serene and peaceful. The trees, folliage, and atmosphere drowned the sound of cars out, even when I was close to the streets. occasionally, birds would chirp (the Red Wattle being very loud), Crows and Magpies were easily heard too. In large bodies of grass such as the grass circle, crickets were heard chirping under the long grass.

I have attatched some sound clips below of what I heard during my time at Royal Park. These were originally video recordings, which I have posted already. I noticed when listening to the sound alone, it's very easy to pick up what could've been missed in the actual video recordings (such as Crows, Magpies, and Crickets chirping).
